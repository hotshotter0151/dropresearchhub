FAQ

We handle your questions

What is DropResearch Hub? +
DropResearch Hub is a product research tool built exclusively for UK ecom sellers and dropshippers. Every week we publish a curated feed of Google Trends verified winning products with full data — margins, saturation scores, grades, supplier links and ad creative tools.
How is this different from Minea or other tools? +
Minea is a great ad spy tool — but it requires you to do the research yourself. DropResearch Hub does the research for you. Every product is already verified, graded and comes with cost prices, margins, supplier links and 3 ready-to-use ad creative scripts. It's a complete end-to-end product launch system, not just a data library.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Absolutely. There are no contracts or commitments. You can cancel at any time from your account. If you cancel you lose the 50% rate — so most members stay subscribed even in months they're not actively testing products.
How often is the product feed updated? +
The feed is updated every Monday morning with fresh products verified by Google Trends data. You'll get a notification when new products drop. All products from previous weeks remain accessible so you can go back and review older picks.
What is the free trial? +
The free trial gives you access to 3 products from the feed, 1 AI research question per day and a preview of the platform. No credit card required. When you're ready to unlock everything — all 47+ products, ad creatives, calculators and store audit — you can upgrade to full access for £49.99 for your first month.
Is this suitable for complete beginners? +
Yes — in fact it's designed with beginners in mind. Every product comes with a launch checklist, step-by-step ad creative briefs and a store audit tool to make sure you're ready before spending on ads. If you're part of our mentorship community you'll already know how to use everything the tool gives you.
